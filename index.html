Mission

Nova Music is a nonprofit corporation that has a mission to give affordable and easily accessible music education to children of all backgrounds. Nova Music's mission is to inspire creativity in budding musicians. Nova Music gives young musicians a chance to grow and learn from experienced student mentors. Putting affordability and accessibility first, the organization removes as many barriers as possible in a student's music education. Headquartered in Woodinville, Washington, Nova Music offers both in-person and online lessons, allowing for flexibility for students and teachers.

Services

Nova Music offers a variety of services and teaches many music related things. With our array of teachers we offer teaching many different lessons on instruments ranging from flute to guitar. We also teach music theory and other essential music knowledge needed for all music students. All of this is at affordable prices and can be both online or in person classes.
