Background

Musical Journey

Pranshu began learning piano at the age of six, starting with lessons at home. Over time, he continued to build on that foundation, steadily growing his skills and musical understanding. His early start and consistent practice have shaped a solid and ongoing connection to the instrument.

Performance Experience

Pranshu has performed in a few recitals, gaining experience presenting music in front of an audience. These performances have helped him develop confidence and stage presence in a supportive setting.

Teaching Philosophy

Pranshu has taught piano to kids in his community as well as his younger brother. He approaches teaching with a focus on sharing what he has learned through his own experience, aiming to help beginners build a strong foundation in a comfortable and encouraging environment.
