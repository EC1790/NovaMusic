Background

Musical Journey

Pranshu began his musical journey at the age of six, starting with lessons at home where he was first introduced to the fundamentals of music. What began as a small, personal exploration quickly developed into a lasting passion. As he continued learning and practicing, he steadily built his skills and expanded his musical understanding. Over time, he progressed from basic exercises to more advanced pieces, gaining confidence and a deeper appreciation for the art.

Performance Experience

Pranshu has had the opportunity to share his music in several recital settings, performing for live audiences. These experiences helped him develop a sense of comfort on stage and taught him the importance of preparation, expression, and presence in a performance setting. Though few in number, each recital gave him a new perspective on performing and connecting with listeners.
