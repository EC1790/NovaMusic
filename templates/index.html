About Us

Mission

Nova Music is a nonprofit making music education affordable and accessible for all children. We believe every young musician should have the chance to explore their passion, grow their skills, and create without financial barriers.

We connect students with skilled peer mentors who inspire, teach, and build confidence. Our teachers are based in Woodinville, Redmond, Bellevue, Issaquah, Sammamish, Bothell, Seattle, and surrounding areas, offering flexible in-person and online lessons to fit each studentâ€™s needs.

Services

Nova Music provides a comprehensive range of music education services, covering essential musical foundations and instrumental instruction. Our skilled mentors and instructors offer lessons across a variety of instruments, including:

  • Piano, violin, flute, guitar, drums, and more
  • Music theory, composition, and ear training
  • Performance techniques and ensemble training

Whether students are beginners seeking guidance or experienced musicians looking to refine their craft, we offer personalized instruction to support their journey.

Our lessons are available both online and in-person, ensuring students can learn at their own pace, from any location. With affordability at the heart of our mission, we strive to keep costs low while maintaining high-quality, engaging, and inspiring music education.
