Jude

Background

Musical Journey

Jude is a multi-instrumentalist with a passion for creativity and collaboration. She began learning piano in 3rd grade, cello in 4th grade, and bass in 8th grade. Since then, She’s been involved in a wide variety of ensembles and musical experiences. Jude currently plays in the WHS Chamber Orchestra, Jazz Bands, and her own independent band. She is also working on a solo music project, exploring songwriting and composition. At Woodinville High School, Jude holds leadership positions as the Public Relations Head and Section Leader in the jazz program, demonstrating her commitment to musical excellence and community engagement.

Performance & Competition Experience

Jude has performed extensively across numerous events, festivals, and venues. Her experience includes appearances with WHS ensembles at Disneyland Music in the Parks, Silverwood Music in the Parks, The Mead Jazz Festival, Northlake Jazz Festival, Northshore Jazz Festival, Newport Jazz Festival, Northshore Orchestra Festival, and the Western Washington Orchestra Festival. In addition to formal competitions, she has played at open mics and WHS’s Night of the Arts, further showcasing her versatility as a performer. Jude brings a wealth of real-world playing experience that informs her musical understanding and teaching.

Teaching Philosophy

Jude’s teaching style is a balance of structure and approachability. While she believes in building a strong technical foundation and fostering discipline, she also emphasizes the joy and accessibility of music. Jude has informally taught piano and is known for her patient, down-to-earth demeanor. She understands that music can feel intimidating at first, so she works to create a space where students feel encouraged, supported, and free to express themselves. Her goal is to help students gain confidence in their playing while having fun and growing in their artistry. Jude believes that professionalism and playfulness can go hand-in-hand in the learning process.
