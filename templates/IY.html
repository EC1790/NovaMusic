Musical Journey

Isaac started playing guitar in 2nd grade, then switched to drums in 4th grade and finally settled on the horn in 9th grade.

Performance Experience

Isaac performed in solo ensemble twice in high school and once in middle school. He has also performed in Benayora and Meanie Hall in Seattle. Over the summer, Isaac will be performing in Portugal across the entire country.

Teaching Philosophy

Isaac likes to teach in a calm and innovative demure, always moving along with new content, but making sure that the students maintain the fundamentals needed to keep their musical journey alive.
