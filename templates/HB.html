Musical Journey

Aditi is a musician with over ten years of experience under her belt, beginning her music journey at 4 with the piano. She switched over to the violin, which she played for over 10 years, and finally switched to the viola, which she has been playing for the past two years.

Performance Experience

Currently, Aditi is an active member of Woodinville High School's Chamber orchestra as a violist, however she continues to play violin in her spare time. She competed in solo and ensemble in the group "Brothers Mun and the Rosin Rascals" and partook in the WSU orchestra festival with her school orchestra.

Teaching Philosophy

Alexandra’s teaching style centers on patience, encouragement, and helping students become independent problem-solvers. She focuses on building a strong foundation of good fundamentals while also fostering creativity and self-confidence. Alexandra tailors lessons to her students’ goals, whether it’s learning the basics, preparing for an audition, or exploring new styles. Her hope is to help students find their voice and a sense of community through music.
