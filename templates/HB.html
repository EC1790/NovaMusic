Henry Birkholz

Background

Musical Journey

Henry has been playing piano for over seven years, with a special love for jazz music and improvisation. His musical journey began with classical foundations, but he quickly gravitated toward the expressive and creative freedom of jazz. Over time, Henry developed strong technical skills and a deep appreciation for groove, harmony, and spontaneous musical ideas. His passion for the piano continues to grow as he explores new musical styles and challenges himself as both a performer and teacher.

Performance & Competition Experience

Henry has performed in monthly recitals across the region for several years, gaining valuable experience playing for live audiences in a wide variety of settings. These performances have helped him build confidence, stage presence, and a strong understanding of how to communicate through music. Whether performing a solo piece or collaborating with others, Henry brings energy and focus to every performance.

Teaching Philosophy

Henry takes a patient and organized approach to teaching. He believes that students thrive when given both encouragement and structure, so he develops clear lesson plans tailored to each student’s goals and skill level. By balancing technical development with creativity and exploration, Henry helps students get the most out of every lesson. His aim is to build a strong foundation while nurturing each student’s individual voice and musical interests.
