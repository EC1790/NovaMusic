Eshaan's enthusiasm for music education led him to establish Nova Music, a nonprofit initiative that aims to bring music instruction to young students. As founder and instructor, he is passionate about creating opportunities for students to explore their musical potential and develop a lifelong appreciation for the arts. Through his engaging and supportive teaching style, Eshaan hopes to inspire the next generation of musicians and empower them to achieve their goals.
