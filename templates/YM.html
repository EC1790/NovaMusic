Advancing Music Education

Throughout his time so far, Yasin has figured that the best way to teach is to try and look at the problem from the student’s point of view. By doing this, the teacher gets a better understanding on what the student needs to work on first before conquering their main problem, and that is where the teacher helps and offers support for their students, ensuring that they have a proper grasp on fundamentals before moving on to bigger challenges.
