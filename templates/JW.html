John Wheelwright

Background

Musical Journey

John has been playing drums for six years, starting his musical journey in 5th grade band. From the very beginning, he found joy and purpose in making music. Drumming has become a central part of his life—not only as a creative outlet but also as a way to connect with others and be part of a community. He views music as a lifelong experience that continues to bring fulfillment and inspiration.

Performance Experience

Throughout his six years in band, John has developed strong ensemble skills and stage presence. He spent two years in marching band, where he learned the importance of timing, coordination, and teamwork. In addition, he has drummed in a band for three years and served as the pit drummer for several musical theater productions. These varied experiences have given him a versatile foundation as both a performer and collaborator.

Teaching Philosophy

John believes in building skills gradually and encouraging every step of progress. His teaching style is supportive and structured, helping students build a strong technical foundation while keeping the learning process fun and motivating. He customizes lessons based on the student’s pace and goals—whether they're just starting out or preparing for performances. John has also taught at the Northshore Summer Music School, where he guided young drummers with enthusiasm and care.
