Musical Journey

Aadi has been playing guitar for about six years, beginning his musical journey through lessons at 4/4 School of Music. What started as a childhood activity has grown into a personal passion, fueled by learning songs and sharing music with family and friends. He continues to play regularly, using the instrument as a creative outlet and a form of self-expression.

Performance Experience

While Aadi does not focus on public performance due to performance anxiety, he has played informally in front of family and friends. His approach to music is centered more on personal growth and enjoyment rather than competition or performance, allowing him to build confidence and skill at his own pace.
