Want to be a teacher?

Nova Music connects passionate student musicians with families seeking affordable music lessons, offering teachers the chance to earn $30+ per hour for paid sessions or gain valuable volunteer hours for community service. You choose your availability, instrument, and whether you want to be paid or volunteer, flexibility is at the core of our program. Join us to share your love of music, build leadership skills, and make a real impact.
